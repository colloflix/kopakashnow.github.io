About Us

Kopa Kash is a Micro-lending product which is provided by M-PAY TECH SERVICES Microfinance bank. Users can download Kopa Kash from Google Play and set up their loan account in seconds, select their preferred M-Pesa account, fill out a set of questions, and get a decision on their loan application in minutes with paperless.
